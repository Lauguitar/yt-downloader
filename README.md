## üé¨ Using yt-downloader

Now that you have installed yt-downloader, here is how to use it:

1. **Open the Application**
   Launch yt-downloader through your applications.

2. **Enter YouTube URL**
   In the main interface, you will see an input box. Copy and paste the YouTube link of the video, audio, or playlist you wish to download.

3. **Select Your Options**
   Choose the quality and format for your download. yt-downloader supports various audio and video formats.

4. **Start the Download**
   Click the Download button. A progress bar will appear, showing the speed and time remaining for the download. 

5. **Access Your Downloads**
   Once completed, your downloaded files will appear in your designated downloads folder. You can easily access them from there.
